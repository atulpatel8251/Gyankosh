### Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
1. What is the SI unit of electric current?
   a) Volt  
   b) Ampere  
   c) Ohm  
   d) Coulomb

2. Which of the following is a good conductor of electricity?
   a) Rubber  
   b) Glass  
   c) Copper  
   d) Wood

3. What is the formula for calculating electric power?
   a) P = IV  
   b) P = IR  
   c) P = V/R  
   d) P = I^2R

4. Which law states that the current passing through a conductor is directly proportional to the voltage across it?
   a) Coulomb's Law  
   b) Ohm's Law  
   c) Faraday's Law  
   d) Kirchhoff's Law

5. What is the resistance of a conductor if the potential difference across it is 10V and the current flowing through it is 2A?
   a) 5 Ohms  
   b) 10 Ohms  
   c) 2 Ohms  
   d) 20 Ohms
